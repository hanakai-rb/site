## Names

Use “Hanakai” to refer to the community and umbrella that houses Hanami, Dry, and Rom. Refer to each project by its own name – Hanami, Dry, Rom – when discussing the project itself.

## Logos

Our logos are available in SVG and PNG. Please preserve the aspect ratio and leave adequate clear space around the mark.

## Colours

Each project has its own accent colour alongside the shared Hanakai palette. Use these only to refer to the projects – don’t repurpose them for unrelated products.

## Permitted uses

You may use the Hanakai and project names and logos to:

- Link to our website or projects
- Credit Hanakai projects you depend on
- Write about Hanakai in blog posts, talks, or articles

## Restrictions

Please don’t:

- Modify the logos or combine them with other marks
- Use our branding in ways that suggest official endorsement without permission
- Use our branding for products or services that compete with our projects

If you’re unsure whether your use is permitted, please get in touch.
